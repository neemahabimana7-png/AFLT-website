AFRILOTT HOLDING LTD

AFRILOTT Holding Ltd was established in 2010. It is specialized in commodity trading, procurement and logistics (goods and Specialized Equipment), supply chain management and IT Solutions.

AFRILOTT is an independent trader in oil and petroleum products, agricultural commodities, and other fast-moving goods. We focus on creating long-term value for our clients by prioritizing their interests reliably, efficiently, and responsibly.

AFRILOTT selects the finest brands available on the market and follows up on all aspects of manufacturing on behalf of our clients.
